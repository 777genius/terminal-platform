# Native Checklist

- Create a native session and verify initial `ready` screen.
- Send input and confirm the focused pane surface updates.
- Create, rename, focus and close tabs.
- Split panes and resize them in both directions.
- Save a session, restore it, and verify topology plus focused pane state.
- Exercise `vim`, `less`, and `fzf`.
- Stress resize churn and confirm subscriptions stay healthy.
- Stop and restart the daemon, then verify stale-client failure is explicit.
